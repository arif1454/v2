DATA WAREHOUSING

In this era of “Data Deluge”, our clients often find it hard to process and manage their data. At V2, we believe in “Making Sense of Data”. By using state of the art data mining technologies (like AWS, Microsoft Power BI, Hadoop, Apache Spark and so on) we help our client to extract concise information from loads of data.

The process includes:
  • Designing and Development of Data Warehouse Solution to store historical data for instant reporting. We work at architectural as well as implementation level
  • Constructing Export- Transform- Loading (ETL) process for data handling, transforming and storing different data stream into single data warehouse
  • Develop Reporting and Daily KPI tool on top of Data warehouse
  • Data validation, Trend Analysis, Anomaly detection in data and Insight mining
  • IT process automation and enhancement for B2C one-to-one engagement application and bulk consumer request/ query management
  • Conduct customized research as per client’s need


With the help of information provided by us, our client can:
  • Initiate Micro Targeting Communication to its consumer base
  • Instant Business Insights Generation can help them to take fast and accurate business decision
  • Trend analysis and forecasting helps to think ahead of time and provides competitive advantages
  • Deep learning helps to find out different patterns

SMASH BOARD

Software that aggregates all queries from different social media channels. Uses AI to auto reply to most of them and uses human agents for the rest. Has all the features required for brand managers, supervisors, agents and customer support team.

This is a Query Management Software. The software is capable of scrapping queries from various social medias ex: Whatsapp, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram etc. These queries then answered by bots. If bots can't recognize, queries are tunnelled towards human agents who are there 24/7 to give support.

Customer satisfaction is the key to boost up revenue for brands. Smashboard helps our clients to reply to customer's questions and give seasonal offers. Smashboard also helps to analyze the queries and classify what offers/services are of great concerned to the user.
